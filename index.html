I am a musician, digital designer and the founder of BookRefine Publishing and its partner boutique record label AudioDefine Records. Having designed for multiple businesses, musicians and authors; I strive to combine all of my skills and provide the best possible experiences.

As a writer and editor, I have been involved with several local news agencies in the past and have written educational reference guides. I completed my Computer Science degree in Sydney, Australia with a focus on cybersecurity and data redundancy.

I enjoy all sorts of art and photography, and at the opposite end of the spectrum I am a classic Saab enthusiast and reptile lover and have a long-standing interest in the paranormal.
